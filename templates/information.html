How do we generate a character?

To start, the site generates a name, age, gender, and country of origin for our character. To randomly generate this information, we use randomuser. To create the short biography, the previous information is fed into the DeepAI Text Generation API. This gives us a couple of random sentences that hopefully make sense in the context. Hobbies are then generated by grabbing the character's age and fetching three hobbies from a large list of hobbies that could potentially be matches. The character has a random song which is generated by picking from Genius's massive catalog of songs using their API. Lastly, a random poem is generated using a random beanpoems poem. By using multiple APIs and sources of information, we can create a truly random, fictional, yet realistic character.
